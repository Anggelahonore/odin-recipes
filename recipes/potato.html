Cheese and Bacon Potato Rounds

Description

An appetizer is a small dish of food or a drink taken before a meal or the main course of a meal to stimulate one's appetite.

Today you found a delisious appetizer made by cooked sliced potatoes in the oven with cheese, and bacon.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium baking potatoes, cut into 1/2 inch slices.

  • ¼ cup melted butter.

  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese.

  • 8 slices bacon - cooked and crumbled.

  • ½ cup chopped green onions.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Brush potato slices on both sides with butter; place them onto the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned and tender when pierced with a knife, 30 to 40 minutes, flipping slices halfway through. Sprinkle with Cheddar, crumbled bacon, and green onions. Continue baking until cheese melts, 3 to 5 more minutes.
